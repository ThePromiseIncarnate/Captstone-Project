Project Sleep Staff

Dr. Akemi Satoyo

Dr. Akemi Satoyo is one of the lead scientists on Project Sleep, brought on by Dr. Rikimaru due to her experience with Eidolons. Her son is the well known philantropist pop idol, Akemi Hoshiki! When she's not working in the lab, she occassionally acts as his manager.

Dr. Kaneko Kazuo

Dr. Kaneko Kazuo is one of the most notable assistant scientists working on the ESL due to his experience with the facility, since his mother was one of the people who worked on it. He has been quite helpful with Project Sleep.

Dr. Maekawa Yui

Dr. Maekawa Yui is the youngest scientist on the ESL, and brings his youthful optimism and unique perspective to Project Sleep. He is deeply loyal to Dr. Rikimaru.

Dr. Rikimaru Yosuke

Dr. Rikimaru Yosuke is the head scientist in charge of Project Sleep, working tirelessly to put his research to the test. He's the youngest scientist on the ESL to be born on Earth who was a child prodigy in the sciences, and is revered for his work. Dr. Rikimaru gets things done.

Dr. Aoki Honoka

Dr. Aoki Honoka is currently the oldest scientist working on the ESL, and her dedication to Project Sleep has been remarkable, with even her husband, Officer Aoki Hideyuu of the Eden Police Force offering himself up on her behalf to get an Eidolon.
